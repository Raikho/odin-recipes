Protein Muffin

Description

This recipe is for a healthy version of a chocolate muffin. It uses Kodiak Cakes pancake mix and whey instead of flour and almond milk instead of milk to have a high protein content without as many carbs or fats.

Ingredients

  • 210g Kodiak Cakes pancake mix
  • 230g Chocolate Whey
  • 65g PB2 Peanut Butter Powder
  • 5 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Almond Milk
  • 1 Tbls Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Whisk dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add Eggs and Almond Milk and mix until barely combined.
  3. Add Choclate Chips.
  4. Put mixture in muffin molds and bake at 350 Degrees for 25 minutes.

Tuna Sandwich

Description

This recipe is a healthy version of a tuna salad sandwhich. It uses tuna without oil and light mayo to have a high amount of protein without as much fat.

Ingredients

  • 1 Can Tuna
  • 1/8 Red Onion
  • 20g Light Mayo
  • 1 Tsp Relish
  • 1 Pinch Salt
  • 2 Slices Wheat Bread

Steps

  1. Combine Tuna, Red Onion, Light Mayo, Relish, and Salt in a bowl.
  2. Spread on Wheat Bread with Lettuce and serve.
